---

An integrated digital & social media marketing campaign repositioning the **Oura Ring** into the FemTech category for women aged 24–35, built around the concept of **body literacy**.

The site covers:

- **Strategy** — the "Synced Life" body-literacy positioning
- **Audience** — ranked purchase drivers and segment insights
- **Channels** — a full-funnel, six-stage customer journey
- **Social & Influencer** — a four-tier creator activation program with sample social copy
- **Creative** — campaign mood board and an influencer-style social video
- **Impact** — KPI framework and budget allocation
